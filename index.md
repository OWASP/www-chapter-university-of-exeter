## Welcome to the OWASP University of Exeter Student Chapter
Helping students and professionals learn and diversify their skills in cyber security.

We are a collection of current, past, and future students and professionals with an interest in the domain. We want to **share and give a platform** for the people to share and learn in a safe environment.
